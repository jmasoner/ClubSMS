## Introduction

ClubSMS is a powerful Android application designed specifically for club organizers, group leaders, and community managers who need to send important messages to large groups efficiently. 

### Key Benefits
- **Time Saving**: Send one message to hundreds of members instantly
- **Cost Effective**: Use your existing SMS plan, no monthly subscriptions
- **Privacy Focused**: All data stays on your device
- **Professional**: Track delivery, manage opt-outs, maintain history
- **User Friendly**: Simple interface that anyone can use

### System Requirements
- Android 5.0 (API 21) or higher
- SMS-capable device
- 50MB available storage
- Access to phone contacts (for import feature)

## Installation Guide

### Step 1: Download the App
1. Download the ClubSMS APK file from the provided source
2. Save it to your Downloads folder or a location you can easily find

### Step 2: Enable Installation from Unknown Sources
**For Android 8.0 and later:**
1. Go to Settings > Security & Privacy > More Settings
2. Enable "Install apps from unknown sources"
3. Or when you tap the APK, Android will prompt you to allow installation

**For older Android versions:**
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Enable "Unknown sources"
3. Confirm the warning dialog

### Step 3: Install the App
1. Navigate to where you saved the APK file
2. Tap on the ClubSMS APK file
3. Tap "Install" when prompted
4. Wait for installation to complete
5. Tap "Open" or find the app in your app drawer

## First Time Setup

### Step 1: Grant Permissions
When you first open ClubSMS, it will request permissions:

**SMS Permission (Required)**
- Purpose: Send messages to your club members
- What we access: Ability to send SMS messages
- What we don't access: We don't read your personal messages

**Contacts Permission (Optional but Recommended)**
- Purpose: Import your club members from phone contacts
- What we access: Names and phone numbers of your contacts
- What we don't access: We don't access other contact data like emails or addresses

### Step 2: Import Your Club Members

#### Option A: Import from Phone Contacts
1. Tap "Import Contacts" on the main screen
2. The app will scan your phone contacts
3. Review the list of contacts found
4. Choose "Import All" to add all contacts, or "Select Specific" for individual selection
5. Confirm your selection

#### Option B: Add Members Manually
1. Tap "Manage Members" 
2. Use the "Add Member" option
3. Enter name and phone number
4. Save the member

### Step 3: Verify Setup
1. Check that your member count shows the correct number
2. Send a test message to yourself or a small group first
3. Verify that messages are being sent successfully

## Contact Management

### Adding New Members

#### Method 1: Import from Phone Contacts
1. Tap "Import Contacts" on main screen
2. Review contacts that have phone numbers
3. Select which contacts are club members
4. Confirm import

#### Method 2: Manual Entry
1. Tap "Manage Members"
2. Tap "Add Member" (+ button)
3. Fill in required information:
   - Name (required)
   - Phone number (required, include country code for international)
   - Email (optional)
   - Notes (optional)
4. Tap "Save"

### Editing Member Information
1. Go to "Manage Members"
2. Find the member you want to edit
3. Tap on their name or use the edit button
4. Update any information
5. Tap "Save" to confirm changes

### Removing Members
1. Go to "Manage Members"
2. Find the member to remove
3. Tap the delete button or use the menu option
4. Confirm deletion
5. The member will be removed from all future messages

### Managing Opt-Outs
When a member asks to stop receiving messages:
1. Find the member in "Manage Members"
2. Mark them as "Opted Out"
3. They will automatically be excluded from future broadcasts
4. You can opt them back in if they request it later

### Member Status Types
- **Active**: Receives all broadcast messages
- **Inactive**: Temporarily excluded from messages
- **Opted Out**: Permanently excluded until status changed

## Sending Messages

### Composing Your Message

#### Message Content Guidelines
- Keep messages clear and concise
- Include essential information: what, when, where
- Use a friendly but professional tone
- Consider your audience and timing

#### Message Length Considerations
- **160 characters or less**: Sent as 1 SMS (most cost-effective)
- **161-306 characters**: Sent as 2 SMS segments
- **307-459 characters**: Sent as 3 SMS segments
- The app shows you exactly how many segments your message will use

#### Sample Messages
**Meeting Reminder:**
"Club meeting tomorrow 7PM at Community Center. Agenda: budget review, upcoming events. Please bring your membership card. See you there!"

**Event Announcement:**

**Emergency Notice:**
"URGENT: Tonight's meeting CANCELED due to weather. Rescheduled to next Thursday same time. Stay safe everyone!"

### Sending Process

#### Step 1: Compose Message
1. Type your message in the text field
2. Check the character count and SMS segment display
3. Review for any typos or missing information

#### Step 2: Review Recipients
1. Check the member count display
2. Ensure you have the right number of active members
3. Remember: opted-out members are automatically excluded

#### Step 3: Send Message
1. Tap "Send to All Members"
2. Review the confirmation dialog showing exact recipient count
3. Tap "Send" to confirm or "Cancel" to abort

#### Step 4: Monitor Progress
1. Watch the status updates as messages are sent
2. The app sends messages in batches to avoid overwhelming your carrier
3. See real-time count of successful and failed deliveries

#### Step 5: Review Results
1. Final statistics will show when complete
2. Check success rate and any failures
3. Failed messages might be due to invalid numbers or carrier issues

### Best Practices for Sending

#### Timing Considerations
- **Business Hours**: 9AM-5PM for official club business
- **Evening**: 6PM-8PM for social events and reminders
- **Avoid**: Early morning (before 8AM) or late night (after 9PM)
- **Weekend**: Use sparingly, only for urgent matters

#### Frequency Guidelines
- **Weekly**: Regular meeting reminders
- **Bi-weekly**: Newsletter-style updates
- **Monthly**: Major announcements and events
- **As needed**: Emergency or time-sensitive information

#### Message Quality Tips
- Proofread before sending (you can't recall SMS messages)
- Include your club name for identification
- Use clear call-to-action when appropriate
- Keep formatting simple (SMS doesn't support rich text)

#### Delivery Status Indicators
- **Excellent (Green)**: 95%+ delivery success
- **Good (Light Green)**: 80-94% delivery success  
- **Poor (Orange)**: 50-79% delivery success
- **Failed (Red)**: Less than 50% delivery success
- **No Recipients (Gray)**: No active members to send to

#### Key Metrics
- **Recipients**: Total number of members who received the message
- **Failed**: Number of delivery failures
- **Success Rate**: Percentage of successful deliveries
- **Message Length**: Character count and SMS segments used
- **Send Time**: When the broadcast was initiated

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Messages Not Sending
**Possible Causes and Solutions:**

1. **Permission Issues**
   - Check that SMS permission is granted
   - Go to Android Settings > Apps > ClubSMS > Permissions
   - Enable SMS permission if disabled

2. **Carrier Limitations**
   - Most carriers limit bulk SMS to 100-200 per hour
   - Split large broadcasts across multiple hours
   - Contact your carrier about business SMS plans

3. **Invalid Phone Numbers**
   - Check member list for incorrect numbers
   - Ensure numbers include country codes
   - Remove disconnected numbers

4. **Network Issues**
   - Check cellular signal strength
   - Try from a different location
   - Wait and retry during off-peak hours

#### Contact Import Problems

1. **No Contacts Found**
   - Grant Contacts permission in Android Settings
   - Ensure contacts have phone numbers saved
   - Check that contacts app is functioning properly

2. **Partial Import**
   - Some contacts may lack phone numbers
   - Check contact format (name and number required)
   - Import remaining contacts manually

#### App Crashes or Errors

1. **Clear App Cache**
   - Go to Android Settings > Apps > ClubSMS
   - Tap "Storage" then "Clear Cache"
   - Restart the app

2. **Free Up Storage**
   - Ensure device has at least 100MB free space
   - Move photos/videos to cloud storage
   - Clear other app caches

3. **Restart Device**
   - Sometimes a simple restart resolves issues
   - This clears memory and resets connections

## Privacy & Security

### Data Protection

#### What Data We Store
- **Member Information**: Names, phone numbers, notes you add
- **Message History**: Content and delivery statistics of messages you send
- **App Settings**: Your preferences and configuration

#### What Data We Don't Store
- **Personal Messages**: We don't read or store your personal SMS
- **Contact Details**: We only access names and phone numbers
- **Location**: No location tracking or storage
- **Usage Analytics**: No behavioral tracking or analytics

#### Where Data Is Stored
- **Local Device Only**: All data stays on your Android device
- **No Cloud Storage**: Nothing is uploaded to external servers
- **No Data Sharing**: Information is never shared with third parties
- **Your Control**: You can export or delete all data anytime

### Security Features

#### Permission Management
- **Minimal Permissions**: Only requests SMS and Contacts access
- **Permission Rationale**: Clear explanation of why each permission is needed
- **Graceful Degradation**: App functions (limited) even without permissions

#### Data Encryption
- **Secure Storage**: Data is stored using Android's secure storage APIs
- **No Plain Text**: Sensitive information is not stored in plain text files
- **App Isolation**: Data is isolated from other apps by Android security

### Privacy Best Practices

#### For Club Organizers
- **Member Consent**: Ensure members have agreed to receive messages
- **Opt-out Respect**: Honor opt-out requests immediately
- **Data Minimization**: Only collect necessary member information
- **Regular Cleanup**: Remove inactive or former members promptly

#### For Message Content
- **Professional Content**: Keep messages relevant to club business
- **No Personal Info**: Don't include sensitive personal information in messages
- **Clear Identification**: Always identify yourself/club in messages
- **Appropriate Timing**: Respect reasonable hours for messaging

### Compliance Considerations

#### SMS Regulations
- **CAN-SPAM Act**: Applies to commercial messages
- **TCPA Compliance**: Requires consent for automated messages
- **Carrier Guidelines**: Follow your carrier's bulk messaging policies
- **International**: Different rules may apply for international messages

#### Best Practices for Compliance
- **Written Consent**: Keep records of member agreement to receive messages
- **Clear Opt-out**: Always provide easy way to stop receiving messages
- **Identify Sender**: Include club name in messages
- **Business Purpose**: Only send messages related to legitimate club business
